Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026  ·  Effective: May 2026

Short version: We collect only what we need to process your order and provide support. We do not sell your data. Payments are handled by Stripe — we never see your card details.

1. Who We Are

Dropless is an Australian consumer products brand operated by Tayfun Simsek, trading as Dropless Brand AR-GE & Manufacturing, based in Wollongong, NSW, Australia.

Contact: info@dropless.com.au
Website: dropless.com.au

This Privacy Policy applies to all personal information collected through our website, online store, and customer communications. We are committed to handling your personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

2. What Information We Collect

We collect personal information only when you actively provide it — for example, when placing an order, contacting us, or subscribing to updates.

  • Order information: name, email address, delivery address, phone number
  • Payment information: processed entirely by Stripe — we do not receive or store your card number, CVV or full payment details
  • Communications: messages you send via the contact form or email
  • Technical data: browser type, device type, approximate location (country/state), and pages visited — collected automatically via Cloudflare for website performance and security

We do not collect sensitive information (such as health, financial or government ID data) and we do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfil your order, including generating shipping labels and dispatch notifications
  • Send order confirmation, shipping updates and customer service communications
  • Respond to enquiries submitted via our contact form or email
  • Comply with legal obligations (e.g. Australian Consumer Law, tax record-keeping)
  • Improve our website and product range based on aggregated, anonymised usage data

We will not use your information for unsolicited marketing without your explicit consent.

4. Third-Party Service Providers

We use trusted third-party services to operate our business. Each provider has its own privacy policy and data handling obligations.

Provider Purpose Data shared
Stripe Payment processing Name, email, order amount. Card details processed directly by Stripe — never stored by us.
Zoho Mail Transactional email (order confirmations, shipping) Name, email address, order details
Cloudflare Website hosting, CDN, DDoS protection IP address, browser data (anonymised)
DigitalOcean Application server (Sydney, Australia) Order and customer data stored on Australian servers
Sendle / Australia Post Parcel delivery Recipient name, delivery address, phone number

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your order data is stored on our application server hosted by DigitalOcean in their Sydney, Australia data centre. We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss or disclosure.

Payment data is tokenised and stored solely by Stripe on their PCI-DSS compliant infrastructure. We retain order records for seven (7) years as required by Australian tax law.

6. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies:

  • Essential cookies: required for the shopping cart and checkout process (set by Stripe)
  • Performance cookies: anonymised traffic data via Cloudflare — no personal identifiers

We do not use advertising cookies, remarketing pixels, or third-party tracking scripts (e.g. Google Analytics, Meta Pixel).

7. Your Rights

Under the Australian Privacy Principles you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate or outdated information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to our legal retention obligations)
  • Complain about how we have handled your information

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@dropless.com.au. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.

8. Children's Privacy

Our products and website are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
