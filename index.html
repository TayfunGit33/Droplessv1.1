Universal Method

Four Steps. Lasting Protection.

⚠️

IMPORTANT: Dropless Protective Line consists of advanced nano-shield sealants, not dirt-removing soaps. Thorough pre-cleaning of the surface is strictly mandatory before application.

1

Clean

Clean the surface thoroughly and make sure it's completely dry.

2

Spray

Apply a light, even mist. Less is more — a thin coat bonds better.

3

Spread

Use a clean microfiber cloth (not the cleaning cloth) to spread evenly.

4

Buff

Buff until clear. Avoid water for 30–60 min. Done.
